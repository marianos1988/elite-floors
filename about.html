About us

We specialize in residential and commercial flooring. As a family-owned business, building relationships with our customers is important. We take care of our community and want to be known for such. We use quality products, offer exceptional service, and have the best warranties for our customers. Each project comes with a 100% satisfaction guarantee that protects you against any labor or product malfunction. We want to change the way contractors are viewed. We are a different kind of contractor – we will always show up when we say we will. We will always quickly deliver on every promise and offer the best product, price, and service in the flooring industry.
